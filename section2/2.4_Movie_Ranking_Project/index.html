The Best Movies According to Roberto

My top 3 movies of all-time.

About Time

This is my favorite movie. The romance, character development, and aesthetics make it a must watch movie.

Howl's Moving Castle

This is a movie by my favorite animation producer Miyazaki Hayao. The character development and magical plot as well as the aesthetics of the movie make it the best animation movie for me.

Parasite

This is an outstanding movie that portrays societal problems within capitalism in a cinematic way.
